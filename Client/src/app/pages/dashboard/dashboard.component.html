Speed Typing

Information

You will be given 100 words of randomly generated text to type out. A timer will start when you begin typing. If you make a mistake, you can simply keep typing and don't need to erase any incorrect input.

When you are finished, your score will be generated, which is the number of characters typed (including spaces) divided by the amount of time taken.

Your top 10 scores can be viewed on the records page.
